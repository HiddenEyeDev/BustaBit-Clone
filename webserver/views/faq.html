Frequently Asked Questions

All the information you need about how to play, deposits, fairness, and more.

Basics

How to play?

Place a bet. Watch the multiplier increase from 1x upwards! Cash out any time to get your bet multiplied by that multiplier. But be careful — the game can bust at any time!

What is a bit?

A bit is a millionth of a bitcoin or 100 satoshis. You can deposit or withdraw bits anytime from your account page.

How can I get free bits?

Every hour you can claim 2 free bits on your account page. These are for testing and fun — feel free to withdraw them anytime.

Deposits

How do I deposit bits?

Transfer bitcoins to the deposit address on your account page. If you need bitcoins, services like Coinbase or LocalBitcoins can help.

How long is the deposit address valid?

Your deposit address is permanent and linked to your account. All payments to it will be credited automatically.

How long do deposits take?

Deposits are credited after one blockchain confirmation — usually within 10 minutes.

Withdrawals

Withdraw bits directly from your account page. Smaller withdrawals are processed instantly, while larger ones may require up to 24 hours.

Fairness

All games are provably fair. See the open-source code on GitHub and join the Bitcoin Talk thread for more technical details.

Gambling Addiction

If you or someone you know may be struggling with gambling addiction, please seek help:

  • UK: gambleaware.co.uk
  • US: ncpgambling.org
  • Self Help: gamblersanonymous.org
